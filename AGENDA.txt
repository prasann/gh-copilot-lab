DevDay Helper — 30 minute session

1) Welcome & Setup — 5m
   Quick hello, what to expect, confirm tools installed (VS Code, Node or browser).

2) Live Demo — 10m
   Show Copilot turning a plain text agenda into JSON and rendering it.

3) Hands-on — 10m
   Attendees pick one small change (title, add presenter) and use Copilot to update JSON and see changes in the page.

4) Q&A — 5m
   Wrap up, share Copilot collection, next steps.
